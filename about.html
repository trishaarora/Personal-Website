TRISHA ARORA
Photographer and Programmer
Trisha Arora is a Norman, Oklahoma native who is currently a Junior at Wesleyan University in Middletown, Connecticut. She is a Mathematics and Computer Science double major and has a passion for new technologies and photography. Trisha has experience in programming in Java, HTML5/CSS, Javascript, and SML/NJ, and is currently learning more coding languages. Using her experiences as well as a knowledge in graphic design, she plans to implement her skills to create beautiful, aesthetic technology.

Her notables in photography include:
-A year as the Photo Editor and 2 years as a photographer for the Wesleyan Argus Newspaper
-A photography class and Exhibition at the University of Oxford in Oxford, England
-A year as a photographer and current Photo Editor of Wesleyan Unlocked Magazine and Calendar
-Contributed to the "So Where are you REALLY From?" Photo campaign, found at wesaasc.org
-Photographer for WesWaves music and arts magazine

After starting her own photography business in high school, Trisha has gained experience in business management, analytics, and budgeting.

With a love for traveling and exploring new cultures, Trisha has backpacked across Europe and India. She has studied abroad at the University of Oxford in Oxford, England, and interned abroad in Dublin, Ireland for the iOS application company WholeWorldBand. You can find her on LinkedIn at www.linkedin.com/in/trishaarora.
